Your duel will follow basic out west rules. A coin will be tossed in the the air. Once it hits the ground you can draw and fire. May the quickest hand and truest shot walk away.~
Instructions (step 1/2):
To start the coin toss press space and keep this pushed until the coin hits the ground with a thut. Then as quickly as possible release the space bar. This is your drawing speed.~
Instruction (step 2/2):
Then try to get the red cursor on the bottom of the screen between the green indicators. The longer you wait the smaller it gets. This is how true your shots goes.
